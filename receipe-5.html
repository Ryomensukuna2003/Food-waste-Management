Vegetarian cheese salad

Cooking time: 20-25 minutes
Serves: 2-3 people

01.

In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, leftover cooked vegetables, chopped onions, green chilies, cumin seeds, coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well to form a filling.

02.

Take a slice of bread and cut it into two triangular halves. Spread a spoonful of the filling on one half of the bread and cover it with the other half. Press gently to seal the edges.

03.

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, gently drop the stuffed bread triangles into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crisp. Flip the bread pakodas occasionally to ensure even cooking.

04.

Once the bread pakodas are golden brown and crisp, remove them from the oil and drain them on a paper towel to remove excess oil and Serve hot with your favorite chutney or ketchup.
