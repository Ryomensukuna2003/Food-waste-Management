Bread Upma

Cooking time: 15-20 minutes
Serves: 2-3 people

01.

Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them crackle.

02.

Add chopped onions, green chillies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions become translucent.

03.

Add chopped tomatoes and sauté until they are soft and mushy and add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.

04.

Add chopped bread pieces and mix everything together until the bread pieces are coated well with the masala. Sauté everything for 5-6 minutes on medium flame until the bread pieces are crispy and lightly browned.

04.

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves (optional) and serve hot.p>
