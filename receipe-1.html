Masala Fried Rice

Cooking time: 20-25 minutes
Serves: 2-3 people

01.

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle Add chopped onions, green chillies and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions become translucent.

03.

Add chopped vegetables - carrots, capsicum, and peas. Sauté for a few minutes until they are cooked.

04.

Add salt, black pepper powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well. Add leftover rice and mix everything together. Make sure the rice is broken down into smaller pieces and is not clumpy.

04.

Mix everything for 5-6 minutes on high flame, until the rice is heated through. Serve hot.
