Paratha Rolls

Cooking time: 20-25 minutes
Serves: 2-3

01.

Take the leftover cooked vegetables and meat and chop them into small pieces. If you don't have any leftover meat, you can skip this step.

02.

Heat a little oil in a pan and add the chopped vegetables and meat. Add salt and pepper to taste and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are heated through and the meat is lightly browned.

03.

Take the leftover parathas and place them on a flat surface. Spoon the cooked vegetables and meat over the paratha, leaving some space at the edges.

04.

Roll the paratha tightly and secure the edges by tucking them in. Repeat the process for all the parathas.
Heat some oil in a pan and place the paratha rolls in the pan. Fry them for a few minutes on each side until they are golden brown and crispy.

04.

Heat some oil in a pan and place the paratha rolls in the pan. Fry them for a few minutes on each side until they are golden brown and crispy. Sprinkle chaat masala over the paratha rolls and garnish with cilantro leaves (if desired).
Serve the paratha rolls hot with ketchup or any other dipping sauce.
