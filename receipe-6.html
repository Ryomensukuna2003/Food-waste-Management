Aloo Paratha

Cooking time: 30-40 minutes
Serves: 4-5

01.

In a mixing bowl, add mashed potatoes, salt, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix everything well.

02.

Add whole wheat flour to the mixing bowl and start kneading the dough. Add water as needed to make a smooth dough. Cover and let the dough rest for 10 minutes.

03.

Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Roll each ball into a small circle.
Place a spoonful of the potato mixture in the center of the circle. Bring the edges of the circle towards the center to cover the potato mixture. Pinch the edges to seal the paratha.

04.

Roll the paratha gently into a round shape. Make sure the potato filling doesn't come out. Repeat the same with the rest of the dough balls.
Heat a tawa or griddle on medium heat. Place the rolled paratha on the hot tawa.

04.

Cook the paratha until you see small bubbles forming on the surface. Drizzle some oil or ghee on top of the paratha.
Flip the paratha and cook the other side until it's golden brown. Drizzle some more oil or ghee on top.

04.

Remove the paratha from the tawa and place it on a plate. Repeat the same with the rest of the dough balls.
Serve the aloo paratha hot with some yogurt or pickle on the side.
